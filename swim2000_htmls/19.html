product details

Updated with a 20% bigger design, the Big Mesh Mummy Backpack is the perfect choice for hauling swim and workout gear. The LBMMB3 features a spacious main compartment for storage and mesh venting for increased dry time and drainage. Over the shoulder straps allow for easy transport, while barrel-lock closures provide quick and easy access to your gear.

Lightweight and functional, this athletic backpack features a side zipper pocket to allow for additional organization and a built-in water bottle pocket to keep hydration at your fingertips. Choose from an array of fun colors to complement your personal style!

Product Features include:
  • Dimensions: 25.19"H x 19"W (2.5 Litres)
  • Updated design - 20% bigger
  • Mesh venting for increased drying time and drainage
  • Barrel lock closure
  • Ideal for carry all of your favorite Tyr Swim Gear and Tyr Goggles
